Important Notes:

  • Buses do not run an afternoon drop off or a morning pickup the last night/morning of camp. The girls spend the night at camp.
  • A Bus Mom will be on each bus for the entire ride as well as at least one aide. A Bus Monitor will load girls and unload girls at each stop and release campers to parents. Parents need to sign in and sign out their camper each day.
  • A camper release form (pdf) is available if someone other than the parent is picking up the camper, or if the camper has permission to walk home from the bus stop.
  • Missed the bus? Find this and more bus-related procedures on the transportation page.
 

Bus Stops and Schedules

Last updated: June 16, 2014

New this Year: We are adding Bus Stop locations!

  • Day Camp will be taking 10 buses to camp this year and each bus will load from a convenient location.
  • To help eliminate confusion and larger stops we will be running only ONE bus from each location.
  • Take a look at all the bus stops below and see which is most convenient for you and your camper.
  • You will have an opportunity to choose your bus stop during the registration process.
  • We will be limiting each bus stop to 65 campers so that we can operate a single bus from any one stop.
  • We have added additional bus stop locations close to popular bus stops.
