Scarf Order Form

Order your unique Day Camp scarf in the color of your choice.  A map of our camp area at Huddart Park is printed right on the scarf  - a great reminder of the fun times you had at camp.  Proceeds from scarf sales help to fund the purchase of new equipment for Day Camp.

Fill out the order form below and send exact cash or a check in the amount of $5 for each scarf ordered made payable to “Peninsula Girl Scout Day Camp”.  Give to your unit leader on the first day of camp.
