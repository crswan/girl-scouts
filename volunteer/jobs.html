IMPORTANT NOTE:

If you signed up as an "At-Home" volunteer, our volunteer coordinator will contact you to discuss your volunteer job. You cannot signup for a job online.

  • Full-Time Unit Leader
  • Full- or Part-Time Program Leader

At-Home Volunteer

  • How it works: If you selected the "at-home job" option when you registered for camp, our volunteer coordinator will email you to sign you up for a job.  Don’t worry if you don’t get an email right away; it takes us several days to figure out what help we need, and then it takes a few more  days  to contact all the volunteers.  WE will contact all at home volunteers in registration order to be fair.
  • All at-home jobs are designed to take approximately 20 hours to complete, but we can't predict exactly how long something will take. When you sign up for a job, you commit to finish the job you started.
  • Some jobs can be done at your own convenience, others must be done on specific days.  Some are before camp, some during and some after camp.
  • Please take a look at the Home Job listing (coming soon) and see which jobs might work for you.  Remember we will email every home job volunteer in registration order and the home job page will be updated several times daily as volunteers select  jobs.
