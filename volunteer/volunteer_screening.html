Volunteer Screening

Updated: April 28, 2014

All at-camp volunteers must complete the Council's Volunteer Screening Process.

Click here to complete the Girl Scout USA Volunteer Screening process. This will take you to the GS Council website, on this page complete:

  • Step 1 - Application
  • Step 2 - Electronic Background Check.
  • Both are large green buttons on the right side of the page.

You have a choice of being finger printed at any business/agency that offers Live Scan fingerprinting. Live Scan finger printing is good forever and costs between $15 - $30 depending on the business/agency you choose. A list of local businesses / agencies that offer Live Scan fingerprinting are listed on the Girl Scout Council website as part of the application process. In addition both the Alameda County and San Jose offices offer free finger printing on certain days and times.

If you prefer, you can choose the option of having a background check instead. The electronic Background check can be done online within the Council’s Volunteer Screening Process.

Question: I've completed the Girl Scouts' screening process before. Do I need to do it again?

Answer:

  • If you have undergone the Girl Scouts' fingerprinting process, that is good forever. You don't need to do anything else.
  • The background-check process is good for 3 years. If you are unsure of your background check date, we can confirm your status for you.
