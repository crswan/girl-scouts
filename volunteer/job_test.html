Watch this space for the 2012 At-Home Jobs list.

Click here to view the list (in Google Docs)

  • How it works: If you selected the "at-home job" option when you registered for camp, our volunteer coordinator will call you to sign you up for a job.
    (Don't worry if you don't get a call right away; it takes us several weeks to figure out what kind of help we need, and then it takes a few more weeks to contact all the volunteers (in order of your registration) and match them up with compatible jobs.)
  • All at-home jobs are designed to take approximately 20 hours to complete, but we can't predict exactly how long something will take. When you sign up for a job, you commit to finish the job you started.
  • Some jobs can be done at your own convenience, while others must be done on specific days.
    • Camp Set-up day: Sunday, July 8, 2012
    • Camp Break-down day: Friday, July 20, 2012
