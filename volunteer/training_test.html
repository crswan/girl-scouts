Required Training for Adult At-Camp Volunteers

Peninsula Day Camp provides free training for all at-camp volunteers.

Training Requirements:

  • First year staff (at our camp) - must attend PDC 1, 4, and either 2 or 3 depending on your job at camp (see Training Schedule).
  • Second year (or more) staff (at our camp) - must attend either PDC 2 or 3 depending on your job at camp (see Training Schedule).
