The 2011 At-Home Jobs list is now available!

  • To apply for an at-home job, register yourself as an adult volunteer and select the "at home job" option. Our volunteer coordinator will call the at-home volunteers in the order their applications are received.
  • All at-home jobs are designed to take approximately 20 hours to complete, but we can't predict exactly how long something will take. When you sign up for a job, you commit to finish the job you started.
  • Some jobs can be done at your own convenience, while others must be done on specific days. See the "2011 Dates to note" section below for pertinent dates.
  • Click here to view the job list.
