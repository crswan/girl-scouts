Bus Stops and Schedules

Last updated: July 28, 2010

Notes:

  • Buses do not run an afternoon drop off or a morning pickup the last night/morning of camp. The girls spend the night at camp.
  • A Bus Mom will be on each bus for the entire ride as well as at least one aide. A Bus Monitor will load girls and unload girls at each stop and release campers to parents. Parents need to sign in and sign out their camper each day.
  • A camper release form is available if someone other than the parent is picking up the camper, or if the camper has permission to walk home from the bus stop.

Watch this space for next year's bus routes and schedules! They should be posted in the spring.
