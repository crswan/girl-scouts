Register Here

For the past few years, Day Camp has been running at maximum capacity. We urge you to apply early to avoid disappointment. Camper applications received with an adult volunteer application are processed immediately. All camper applications without an adult volunteer will be placed on the waiting list in the order they are received. These campers will be removed from the waiting list as additional adult volunteers are recruited to help at camp. For more information, go to the Volunteer Page.
