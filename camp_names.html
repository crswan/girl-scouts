Camp Names

Updated: June 8, 2014

A time-honored tradition among Peninsula Day Camp aides and adult volunteers is the selection (and guarding) of your camp name. Campers try to trick the aides into revealing their real names, and on the last morning of camp they find out whether they guessed correctly when the aides' real names are revealed at last. No fair writing them down!
