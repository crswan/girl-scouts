At-Camp Volunteer Training Information & Signup

The on-time registration deadline for Day Camp 2011 has passed.

  • Campers registering after the April 1 deadline will be charged a $50 late-registration fee and will be put on a waitlist while we recruit unit leaders. We will contact campers in registration order as we are able to remove them from the wait list. If you can register as a full time at camp volunteer your campers will automatically be accepted into camp. If you have questions please contact debbie@peninsuladaycamp.org. Visit our online registration page now.
  • Important note for Aides: This year we are expecting a large number of returning aides, along with a large class of new Aides from last year's AIT program. As a result, we are not accepting late registration for aides.
    Note: there is no cancellation fee for aides who register but need to cancel later.
  • We are actively recruiting Unit Leaders.  No experience needed; training will be provided.  For more information about how (and why) to volunteer at camp, visit our volunteers page.
