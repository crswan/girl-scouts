Refund Policy

Cancellations that are received prior to May 1st will receive a full refund. Cancellations that are received on or after May 2 and prior to June 1 will receive a full refund minus a $50 administrative/program fee. This fee is because we will have purchased materials, used postage, printed forms and made reservations based on your camper attending camp. There will be no refunds for cancellations received on or after June 1.
